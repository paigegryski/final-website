About

Hey there! My name is Paige, and I'm a third-year Advertising and Web Design student at the University of Florida. I grew up on Old Bay and lacrosse in Columbia, Maryland. I have always had a passion for creating, and when I got my hands on Photoshop for the first time during my freshman year of college, I knew I didn’t want it to just be a hobby.

I have the patience and politeness of a waitress and the open mind of someone who has worked in the performing arts industry. Thanks to my time in charge of a sorority recruitment committee, I can go days on end without sleep and still demonstrate sound judgment and strong leadership. Within me is the determination of a dedicated citizen who sees flaws in the world around her and does not recoil in fear, but thinks of ways to fix them. I have the loud, creative mind of an artist.

On this website, you’ll find my resume, some work that I’ve done, and ways you can contact me.

Happy Scrolling!
